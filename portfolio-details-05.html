Simphony Kiosk

Client : Oracle Product
Category : UX Case Study
Date : 15/December/2021
Product : Simphony

Simphony Kiosk is a product that caters to a self-ordering experience, which deals with the end to end food ordering. The Business goal was to increase orders, influence consumer sales, and increase consumer engagement with different targetted groups. A few of the targetted groups were quick-service restaurants, limited-service hotels, fast casual, and stadiums.

As a part of this design process, I have understood the requirement and conducted the initial interviews, and done the competitive analysis. Then built a sitemap that described the entire journey in terms of different phases like pre-ordering, ordering, and post-ordering phase. After that built the low-fidelity wireframes and did the user testing. and Further worked on high-fidelity wireframes.
