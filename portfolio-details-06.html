Improving the Entire Navigational Flow in the Companies Portal

Client : Recur
Category : SaaS
Date : 30/March/2023
Website: : www.app.recur.club

The team has decided to build a single platform to fully maintain Back Office, Point of Sale (POS), Enterprise Management Console (EMC) users, and their access. The entire back office software is built on a different architecture (OJET-6) and the Enterprise Management Console software was upgraded to a new OJET-12 which supported a complete stack of components to make for a new redwood design system.

Problem Statement (‘How might we’ statement)

How might we help an administrator add and manage user access of all employees across the Back Office, EMC, and POS from a single unified platform?
