Taxes - Frontline Manager(Web EMC)

Client : Oracle Product
Category : Enterprise UX
Date : 5/September/2022
Product : Frontline Manager

The taxes Module is a part of Enterprise Resource Management, which includes tax rates, tax class, and tax Parameters. In this, the administrator adds and configures tax rates and tax classes to calculate taxes for the menu items which they are selling.

As a part of this redesign process, I analyzed the legacy UI and understood the complete module by understanding the user goals and Shape of the data. Further worked on the low-fidelity wireframes and built a flow. After that updated the flow based on the feedback received from the product owners. And finally built the high-fidelity flow.
